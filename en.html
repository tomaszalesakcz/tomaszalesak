Frequently Asked Questions

Do you only focus on email marketing?
I view email as part of retention marketing. That's why I also focus on customer data analysis, advanced segmentation, and communication workflows across channels like SMS, loyalty programs, push notifications, and direct mail. The goal is to keep customers engaged longer and encourage repeat purchases.
What if I'm not using email marketing at all yet?
We'll start from scratch – from choosing the right tool, through strategy, to your first automations and campaigns. Your email marketing will grow alongside your e-commerce business.
When will I see the first results?
The first visible results typically appear within the first few weeks after launch. With long-term collaboration, the effect multiplies over time as we gather more data.
Can you help me with other areas of marketing?
While I have knowledge of other areas of online marketing, my main focus remains retention marketing. However, I'm happy to recommend proven colleagues for other services.
